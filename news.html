The Office of Guidance Counseling and Services leads the World Mental Health Celebration every 10th day of October. The theme for this year's celebration is "Mental Health is a Universal Right", as presupposed by the World Foundation of Mental Health. The mental health campaign and awareness program are scheduled from October 9 until 13, 2023, on all Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College (ISPSC) campuses. This day, October 10, 2023, the celebration of the program takes place at the ISPSC Santa Maria Campus College Gymnasium. This program features the ever active resource speaker, Mrs. Princess D. Racca, RGC - Head of Guidance Counseling and Services. With the participation of the College of Computing Studies (CCS) student leaders, classroom officers, and the officers of the Computing Society of the Philippines (CSP) of Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College, Santa Maria Campus, they actively participate in spreading awareness about mental health and making a positive change towards mental health.
