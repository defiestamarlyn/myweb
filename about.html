ORDINARY GIRL

By: Marlyn Rose V. Defiesta

I am an ordinary student of Ilocos Polytechnic State College / University of Ilocos Philippines who faces despite so many challenges, I has been able to be happy every day. I'm young woman poured out heart and soul in love with my friends and family, instilling in my values of determination, kindness, and perseverance. Life is a journey filled with dreams, aspirations, and the pursuit of success. I am a girl who, dreams of achieving my goals and making my parents proud. I am committed to learning and growing. I believe that every individual has unique talents and skills, and I am determined to discover and nurture my own. I am determined to graduate and find a fulfilling job to repay my parents for their sacrifices. My parents have always been my biggest supporters. They have made countless sacrifices to ensure that I have the best opportunities in life. From funding my education to providing emotional support, they have been there every step of the way. Their sacrifices inspire me to work harder and achieve my dreams.
